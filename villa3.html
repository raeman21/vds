About this space

The Studio has a large garden with Lake Victoria Lake front. 2 minutes walk to the beach. It is 20 minutes to Kisumu Airport and 25-30 minutes to Kisumu. Great for bird-watching, peace and quiet. Great when solo or accompanied. Has WIFI, cable TV. A washer is available. On site secure parking.
