About this space

A very cozy 1 bedroom house with a Lakefront. A gated community with 24-hr security. 30 mins to Kisumu, 20 mins to the Airport. You get city-village ambiance. Great for peace & quiet & bird-watching. Enjoy an exquisite view of morning sunrise, Kisumu town by night & Villa Del Sol Boat ride. WIFI, Cable TV, Air-Conditioner, washer and Gazebo available. Great for marriage proposals and Honey moons. A cook is available on request with additional cost. A restaurant on site.

The space

In response to Covid-19, we now provide enhanced cleaning. Our space has been disinfected and sanitized. We provide an automated hand sanitizer to keep your safety. To add to your enjoyment at Villa Del Sol, we provide 2 boats: a cruise boat and a speed boat available for hire for visits to different Lake Victoria islands, or for picnics, bird-watching, etc. Coming to Villa Del Sol is a great getaway from the hassles and bustle of life. Great for a quiet honeymoon/ or rest. Expansive area to sit by the Lake and enjoy the breeze, hold a Lakeview wedding and picnic. The Villa has two double beds. You will also have a vast compound for your enjoyment

Guest access

View of Lake Victoria and Villa Del Sol beach. Paga beach is 4 minutes walking distance. Excursions can be organized to: Maboko, Ndere, Rusinga Island and Kisuri Beach (Mfangano Island). Visit President Obama's home and Obama's Sauti Kuu Sports, resource and vocational Training Center (45 minutes away), Kakamega Forest (45 minutes away). Bring your car or hire one. A taxi, tuk tuk and motor cycle available on request at discounted rates.
