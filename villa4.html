About this space

This Lakefront apartment is a romantic facade that appeals to love-birds. Breeze comes in from the living room & bedrooms' terrace. A gated community with 24 hr. security. 30 mins to Kisumu, 20 mins to the airport. Near CDC/KEMRI. Has city-village ambiance. Great for peace & quiet, bird-watching, marriage proposals & honeymoons. Enjoy morning sunrise, Villa Del Sol boat-rides, fishing & the beach restaurant. WIFI, Cable TV, Air-conditioners & washer. A Cook is available with additional costs.

The space

Villa (4) Del Sol occupies the downstairs apartment of the beautiful house facing Lake Victoria. The living room, kitchen and dining are part of an open plan with modern appliances including a hob. Bring your car or hire one. A taxi, tuk tuk and motor cycle available on request at discounted rates. In response to Covid-19, we now provide enhanced cleaning. Our space has been disinfected and sanitized. We provide an automated hand sanitizer to keep you safe from Covid-19. To add to your enjoyment at Villa Del Sol, we provide 2 boats: a cruise boat and a speed boat available for hire for visits to different Lake Victoria islands, or for picnics, bird-watching, etc. Coming to Villa Del Sol is a great getaway from the hassles and bustle of life. Great for a quiet honeymoon/ or rest. Expansive area to sit by the Lake and enjoy the breeze, hold a Lakeview wedding and picnic.
