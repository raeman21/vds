About this space

A very cozy 1 bedroom villa, located within a gated lakefront community with 24- hour security. 30 mins to Kisumu, 20 mins to Kisumu Airport. You get city-village ambiance. Great for peace & quiet & bird-watching. Enjoy an exquisite view of the sunrise. Enjoy a VDS Cruise boat-ride at an additional fee. Local fish eatery at VDS Beach restaurant. WIFI, Cable TV, Air-Conditioner, washer, available. Great for marriage proposals & honeymoon. A Cook is available on request with additional cost.

The space

Villa (2) of the Villa Del Sol property has a Lakefront. Enjoy the lake breeze while staying at Villa Del Sol. You will enjoy an exquisite view of morning sunrise, Kisumu town by night. Local boat riding and local fish eatery available at Paga Beach house just 3 minutes walk away. WIFI, cable TV, Air-conditioner, washer and Gazebo available. Support local eateries by having a meal at Paga beach house.

Guest access

Guests can access all of the space. You will also have a vast compound for your enjoyment
