About this space

This upstairs apartment has an excellent Lake view & breeze. Located in a gated lakefront community, it has 24-hr security. It is 30 mins to Kisumu, 20-25 mins to Kisumu airport. Near CDC/KEMRI. Has city-village ambiance. Great for peace & quiet, bird-watching, marriage proposals. WIFI, Cable TV, air-conditioner, washer. Enjoy Villa Del Sol boat ride & fishing. Great beach restaurant and picnic area. A cook is available on request with additional costs. A beach restaurant is available.

The space

This upstairs Villa is more spacious than the ground floor apartment. Villa (5) Del Sol has an excellent view of Lake Victoria and Kisumu. 2 modern bedrooms. An open plan in the living room, dining and kitchen. The latest kitchen appliances including a hob. To add to your enjoyment at Villa (5) Del Sol, we provide 2 boats: a cruise boat and a speed boat available for hire for visits to different Lake Victoria islands (Maboko island, Ndere Islandor for picnics, bird-watching, etc. Coming to Villa Del Sol is a great getaway from the hassles and bustle of life. Great for a quiet honeymoon/ or rest. Expansive area to sit by the Lake and enjoy the breeze, hold a Lakeview wedding and picnic.
